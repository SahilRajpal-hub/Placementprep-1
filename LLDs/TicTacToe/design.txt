Game
Controls the game loop.

Manages the board and players.

Switches turns.

Checks for game status.

Player
Has a name and a symbol (X or O).

Makes a move on the board.

Board
Maintains the grid state.

Validates and places moves.

Checks for win or draw.

GameStatus
Enum to represent state of the game.
